About Me

I am a geospatial scientist with over 7 years of international experience applying GIS, remote sensing, and LiDAR to tackle critical challenges in climate resilience, hydrology, and urban planning. My work is driven by a passion for leveraging technology to create practical and innovative solutions.

I have a strong publication record and advanced programming skills, with practical field expertise across global environments. I am recognized by USGIF and multiple research bodies for my innovation in geospatial analytics. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD in Transportation & Environmental Engineering at Florida Atlantic University, building on my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in Civil and Geomatics Engineering.
