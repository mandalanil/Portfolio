My skills

My creative skills & experiences.

My creative journey began post my undergraduate studies at Tribhuvan University in Nepal, where I embarked on a fulfilling role as a GIS Associate at Naxa Pvt Ltd, a GeoICT company. Over five years, my responsibilities evolved from intensive UAV data collection and processing to intricate information extraction. A pivotal transition led me to collaborate closely with the system development team, resulting in the creation of numerous Python scripts for WebGIS applications. During this time, I engaged with international professors, co-authoring several published papers.
